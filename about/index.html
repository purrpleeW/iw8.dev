About IW8-Mod

IW8-Mod is a game modification for Call of Duty®: Modern Warfare® (2019) that allows for offline and decentralized play.

Goals

  • Provide users with LAN (P2P) multiplayer.
  • Revive lost content that Activision won't.
  • Allow for custom mods (GSC) offline without risking accounts.

Developers

  • xifil
  • luxploit

Project Assists

  • .r4v3n - provided fix for Arxan bypass, ingame bots, loadout save/load ideology (raw DDL buffer saving instead of manual property saving), contributed to LAN.
  • purrplee - assist developer, OG launcher developer, contributed to LAN
  • Ezz-lol/boiii-free - general codebase
  • mallgrab - Arxan bypass for Black Ops: Cold War (CWHook)
  • momo5502 - Arxan/Steam research, former lead developer of XLabsProject
